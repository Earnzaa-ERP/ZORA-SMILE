Questions, answered

Everything you need to know.

Hydrogen peroxide (the active whitening agent), potassium nitrate (calms tooth nerves), glycerin (carrier), water, sodium hydroxide (pH balancer), and natural mint flavor. That's it. No parabens, sulfates, dyes, or fluff.
Brush teeth, dry them with a clean towel, peel one strip per arch and press onto teeth. Wait 30 minutes, peel off, rinse. Once a day for 14 days. Don't eat or drink anything but water during the wear.
Original is our standard formula and works for most people. Pick Sensitive if you've ever had pain from a whitening product before, if you have receding gums, or if it's your first time whitening. Sensitive uses half the peroxide and slows the timeline by ~3 days.
A typical 14-day cycle holds for 4–6 months depending on diet (coffee, wine, tea darken faster). The pen and toothpaste extend that significantly. Most customers cycle the strips twice a year.
Manufactured in FDA-registered, GMP-certified facilities in the US. Every batch is independently lab-tested for peroxide concentration, pH stability, and microbial safety. Reviewed by our cosmetic dentist advisory board. Vegan, cruelty-free, gluten-free.
