How it works

Charcoal that targets the deep stuff.

For the stains that toothpaste can't budge.

Activated charcoal works by adsorption — surface stains literally bind to the charcoal particles and lift away when you rinse. It's chemistry, not scrubbing. Our particle size is fine enough to be gentle on enamel.
Especially effective on the dark, settled-in marks that come from years of coffee, red wine, black tea, or smoking. Where toothpaste runs out of room, charcoal keeps going.
Don't use it daily. Charcoal is a deep-clean, not an everyday product. Two or three times a week is plenty — the rest of the time, your regular WhiteBoost toothpaste handles the lighter work.
Our charcoal comes from sustainably sourced coconut shells, activated at high temperature in a US facility. No fillers, no synthetic blacks, no clay. Just pure activated charcoal and a touch of mint.
