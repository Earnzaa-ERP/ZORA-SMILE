What's inside

A daily routine, dialed in.

Four things every Zora tube does, twice a day.

Micro-polish particles gently buff away the daily film of coffee, wine, and tea before it sets in. No abrasion, no enamel damage — just steady upkeep.
Sodium fluoride strengthens enamel and helps prevent cavities. Our formula is pH-balanced to avoid the demineralization that some "whitening" toothpastes cause.
Zinc citrate neutralizes the sulfur compounds that cause morning breath. The result lasts longer than a quick mint — you'll feel it through lunch.
No SLS (the foaming agent linked to canker sores), no parabens, no artificial dyes, no microplastics. Vegan, gluten-free, and dentist-tested.
