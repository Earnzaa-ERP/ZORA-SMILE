Our story

Born in a dentist's chair, refined in a lab.

Our founders were patients before they were product builders. After years of trying drugstore strips, gel kits, and in-office sessions, the pattern was always the same: results that came with sensitivity, or no real results at all.

So we partnered with a small group of cosmetic dentists and formulators to build something different. Two years of testing later, Zora Smile launched with one promise: visible results without sacrificing your enamel or your weekend.

Today we ship to over a dozen countries, but we still answer every customer email ourselves.

What we believe
